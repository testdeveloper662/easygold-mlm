1. Subscription
(a)The undersigned investor (the “Subscriber”) hereby irrevocably subscribes for and agrees to purchase EASYGOLD Tokens (the “Tokens” or “Securities”) of Hartmann & Benz, LLC, a District of Columbia limited liability company (the “Company”), at a purchase price of $1.00 per Token (the “Per Security Price”). The minimum subscription amount is $500 (i.e., 500 Tokens) for Accredited Investors (as defined in Rule 501 of Regulation D under the U.S. Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the “Securities Act”)), or $500 (500 Tokens) in transactions made outside the United States to persons who are not “U.S. Persons” (as defined in Regulation S under the Securities Act), pursuant to Regulation S.
(b)The Subscriber understands that the Securities are being offered pursuant to a private placement memorandum dated November 15, 2022 (the “Memorandum”), and that the Securities have not been and will not be registered under the Securities Act. By executing this Subscription Agreement, the Subscriber acknowledges that the Subscriber has received this Subscription Agreement, a copy of the Memorandum (including all exhibits thereto), and any other information that the Subscriber has requested or deemed necessary to make an informed investment decision.
(c)The Subscriber’s subscription may be accepted or rejected, in whole or in part, by the Company in its sole discretion at any time. The Company may also decide, in its sole discretion, to allocate to the Subscriber only a portion of the Tokens that the Subscriber has subscribed for. The Company will notify the Subscriber whether the subscription has been accepted (in whole or in part) or rejected. If the Subscriber’s subscription is rejected (in whole or in part), the Subscriber’s payment (or the applicable rejected portion of such payment) will be returned without interest, and all obligations of the Subscriber under this Agreement with respect to that rejected portion shall terminate. The Subscriber acknowledges and agrees that: (i) any returned funds may be returned in U.S. Dollars, regardless of the form of the Subscriber’s initial payment; (ii) if the Subscriber paid with gold bars accepted by the Company, the Company is authorized to convert such gold into U.S. Dollars at the then-applicable conversion rate at any time after receipt; and (iii) if any payment in gold bars is converted into U.S. Dollars, the Company will not be liable for any fluctuation in the price of gold during the period in which the Subscriber’s funds (or the proceeds thereof) were held by the Company or on the Company’s behalf.
(d)The aggregate number of Tokens offered for sale shall not exceed 600,000,000 Tokens (the “Maximum Offering”). The Company may continue to accept subscriptions for the Tokens until the earliest of: (i) the date on which the Maximum Offering has been fully subscribed; (ii) the date on which a registered offering under Regulation A+ is qualified by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”); or (iii) the date on which the Company elects, in its sole discretion and in accordance with applicable SEC regulations, to terminate or extend this offering. This offering of Tokens is being conducted on a “best efforts” basis pursuant to Rule 251(d) (3)(i)(F) of Regulation A, meaning there is no guarantee that any minimum amount of Tokens will be sold.
(e)If this subscription is rejected in its entirety, or if the sale of the Securities (or any portion thereof) is not consummated for any reason, then this Subscription Agreement shall be void and of no further force or effect, except that Section 5 (Survival of Representations and Indemnity) shall remain in effect.
2. Purchase Procedure
The full purchase price for the subscribed Tokens must be paid by the Subscriber at the time the Subscriber signs and delivers this Subscription Agreement to the Company. Payment shall be made in immediately available funds by wire transfer, ACH, delivery of gold bars (as permitted by the Company), or other method approved by the Company (which may include payment in cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin or Ether to a wallet address designated by the Company), using the payment instructions provided by the Company.
