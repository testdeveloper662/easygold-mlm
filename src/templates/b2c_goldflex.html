1.0 Subscription for GoldFlex Investment Plan
1.1 Subscription
1.1.1 The Investor hereby subscribes to the GoldFlex investment plan as set out in this Agreement and agrees to commit capital to the Company (the “Capital Commitment”) according to the plan selected under Section 7.
1.1.2 The Investor shall pay the full Capital Commitment on the date of execution of this Agreement via certified cheque, bank transfer, or other mutually agreed payment method.
1.2 Option of Shares Security for Investment
1.2.1 The Investor acknowledges that the GoldFlex investment is primarily backed by real gold and other precious metals assets.
1.2.2 In the unlikely event that the Company is unable to pay the Investor their due ROI under the GoldFlex Plan, the Investor’s investment shall be secured by the Company’s shares, which shall crystallize only to satisfy the outstanding ROI and investment obligation.
1.2.3 For the purposes of this Agreement, “Crystallization” means the conditional activation and application of the Company’s shares as a secondary security mechanism solely upon the occurrence of the Company’s failure to pay the Investor the due Return on Investment and/or the invested capital when due under the applicable GoldFlex Plan. Upon such Crystallization:
a.
the shares shall become enforceable only to the extent necessary to satisfy the outstanding unpaid ROI and/or invested capital;
b.
the Investor shall not be deemed a shareholder of the Company unless and until such enforcement is completed;
c.
the Crystallization shall not confer any voting rights, management rights, or dividend entitlements on the Investor beyond what is strictly required to satisfy the outstanding obligation; and
d.
once the outstanding obligation is fully satisfied, the Crystallization shall automatically lapse and the Investor shall have no further claim to the Company’s shares.
1.2.4 For the purposes of any Crystallization under this Agreement, the Parties acknowledge and agree that the Company’s shares shall be valued at Five United States Dollars (US$5.00) or its Nigerian Naira equivalent per share.
1.2.4.1 Any application, allocation, realization, or enforcement of such shares pursuant to Crystallization shall be calculated strictly on the basis of this agreed valuation and shall be limited to the number of shares required to satisfy the outstanding unpaid Return on Investment and/or invested capital owed to the Investor at the time of Crystallization.
1.2.5 Initiation of Crystallization by Notice
Crystallization of the Company’s shares as a security remedy under this Agreement shall occur only upon written notice issued by the Investor to the Company (a “Crystallization Notice”) following the Company’s failure to pay any due Return on Investment and/or invested capital when due under the applicable GoldFlex Plan. Such Crystallization Notice shall:
a.
specify the nature and amount of the outstanding obligation;
b.
state the Investor’s intention to invoke the Crystallization remedy under this Agreement; and
c.
allow the Company a reasonable cure period of fourteen (14) calendar days from receipt of the Crystallization Notice to remedy the default
1.
Where the Company fails to remedy the default within the cure period, the Crystallization shall become effective automatically upon the expiration of such period, and same shall become enforceable on the Company by the Investor.
1.2.6 Share Transfer and Statutory Filings Upon Crystallization
Upon the effective Crystallization of the Company’s shares pursuant to this Agreement, the Company shall, within thirty (30) calendar days from the expiration of the applicable cure period:
a.
take all necessary corporate actions to effect the transfer, issuance, or realization (as applicable) of the required number of shares to the Investor or its nominee, strictly to the extent required to satisfy the outstanding unpaid Return on Investment and/or invested capital;
b.
execute and deliver all share transfer instruments, resolutions, registers, and ancillary documents required to give full legal effect to such transfer or realization; and
c.
complete all requisite regulatory and statutory filings, including filings with the Corporate Affairs Commission or any other relevant authority, in accordance with applicable law.
d.
The Investor shall bear all costs, fees, and expenses associated with the share transfer and statutory filings under this Clause.
